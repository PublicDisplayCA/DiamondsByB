ABOUT DIAMONDS BY B

Diamonds By B started when the Founder Brent was looking for an engagement ring for his now wife Michelle. He quickly realized that information and guidance was lacking from different retailers which left him overwhelmed and confused. That was until a chance encounter led him to his now business partner and mentor Wayne.

After purchasing an engagement ring, he started to reflect on how he could help guide others through the process of engagement ring shopping. This gave Brent the idea to start his own company. He wanted to ensure that as much guidance and support could be provided for those looking to purchase an engagement ring or any type of jewellery they had in mind.

The process: how it works

Whether you are looking for a custom-made engagement ring, wedding band or an everyday piece to gift yourself or for someone special, we keep our commitment to provide the same quality service. We highly recommend scheduling in person consultations for customized pieces as this will take some time, where our expert team can provide suggestions and go over the fine details of what you are looking for and answer any questions along the process.

  • Our first step

    is understand what you are looking for so that our team can create some design ideas to suit your needs. Whether it’s a classic, modern or Custom Unique design, Diamonds by B is here to help you make your dreams turn into reality. to create some ideas of designs you would like, whether it's a classic, modern or custom unique design.

  • Second step

    is to find gemstones that will accentuate your jewellery. We provide a wide range of selections, whether it's a Diamond, Ruby, Topaz, Birthstones or any other fine gems to match any occasion!

  • Third step

    is to have our team create a 3D sketch of your design with all the fine details for your review and approval. Our goal is to have our clients involved in the creative process.

  • Fourth step

    , After details are approved, we will source the stones and start the process by having our team of talented goldsmiths and gem setters bring your visions to life. Once you have approved the design the stones will be sourced to our team of talented goldsmiths and gem setters will follow the 3D design and start to create your piece.

  • Final step

    we will schedule an in person appointment with you to review the finished product.
