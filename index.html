ABOUT DIAMONDS BY B

Diamonds By B started when the founder Brent was looking for an engagement ring for his now wife Michelle. He realized quickly that the guidance and information throughout the process was lacking from the big format retailers which left him overwhelmed and confused. That was by chance, he encountered his now business partner and mentor Wayne.

After purchasing an engagement ring, he started to reflect on how he could help guide others through the process of engagement ring shopping. This gave Brent the idea to start his own company. He wanted to ensure that as much guidance and support could be provided for those looking to purchase an engagement ring or any custom jewellery they had in mind.

The process: How it works

Whether you are looking for a custom-made engagement ring, wedding band or an everyday piece to gift yourself or for someone special, we keep our commitment to provide the same quality service. We highly recommend scheduling an in person consultation for customized pieces as this will take some time, where our expert team can provide suggestions and go over the fine details of what you are looking for and answer any questions along the process.

  • Our first step

    is understand what you are looking for so that our team can create some design ideas to suit your needs. Whether it’s a classic, modern or custom unique design, Diamonds By B is here to help you make your dreams turn into a reality.

  • Second step

    is to source gemstones and create a rough design that will accentuate your jewellery. We will schedule an appointment to view the design and gems. We provide a wide range of selections, whether it's a Diamond, Ruby, Topaz, Birthstones or any other fine gems to match any occasion!

  • Third step

    is to have our team create a 3D sketch of your design with all the fine details for your review and approval. Our goal is to have our clients involved in the creative process.

  • Fourth step

    takes your approved design forward to our talented goldsmiths and gem setters. Our team follows the specs and works to bring bring vision to life!

  • Final step

    is when we schedule an in-person appointment to have the big reveal!
